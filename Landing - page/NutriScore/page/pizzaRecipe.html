Pizza

An Italian dish consisting of a round flat dough baked with tomato sauce and cheese, usually with additional toppings such as meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 200 g ready-to-use bolognese sauce
  • 150 g grated mozzarella cheese
  • 100 g red tomatoes, cut crosswise into 2 parts, sliced ​​0.5 cm thick
  • 100 g fresh button mushrooms, sliced ​​0.5 cm thick, sauté until wilted
  • 450 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp (4 g) salt
  • 300 ml water
  • 1½ tsp (6 g) instant yeast
  • 1½ tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 100 g onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 50 g red bell pepper, diced small
  • 300 g ground beef
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp coarse ground black pepper


How to Make

  • Dough: Place all ingredients in a mixing bowl, knead until smooth. Cover the dough with plastic wrap or a clean cloth, let it rest until it doubles in size (90-120 minutes).
  • Deflate the dough, divide it into 3 equal parts. Wrap each dough with plastic wrap, store in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Set aside.
  • Toppings: Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant. Add red bell peppers and green bell peppers, cook until fragrant.
  • Add ground beef, oregano, basil, salt, and coarse black pepper, cook while stirring until the meat is cooked through. Remove from heat.
  • Assembly: Take 1 piece of pizza dough, roll it out with a rolling pin until it forms a circular shape with a diameter of 20 cm, or press the pizza dough with fingertips to form the desired size. Place the dough on a flat baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Brush the surface of the dough with 2-3 tbsp tomato sauce, leaving a 1 cm edge of the dough uncovered. Add 2-3 tbsp of the sautéed meat on top. Sprinkle with ⅓ of the mozzarella cheese, arrange tomato slices and sautéed mushrooms on top of the cheese.
  • Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at 200℃ until cooked and the edges of the dough are golden brown (10-15 minutes). Remove from the oven, cut into slices as desired. Serve immediately.
