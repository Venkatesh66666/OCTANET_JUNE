Smoothie

A smoothie is a thick and creamy beverage made from pureed raw fruit, vegetables, and sometimes dairy products, typically using a blender.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)


How to Make

  • Place the banana, frozen berries, Greek yogurt, spinach, chia seeds, and almond milk in a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy. If the smoothie is too thick, add more almond milk until desired consistency is reached.
  • Taste and add honey if additional sweetness is desired. Blend again to mix.
  • Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
