Salad

A dish consisting of a mixture of small pieces of food, typically with vegetables or fruit. Salads are usually served at room temperature or chilled, with a variety of dressings and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: nuts, seeds, or croutons for added crunch


How to Make

  • In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, shredded carrots, and red onion.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  • Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat the vegetables evenly.
  • Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top of the salad.
  • Add any optional toppings like nuts, seeds, or croutons if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh, healthy salad!
