Burger

A sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun, often accompanied by cheese, lettuce, tomato, bacon, onion, pickles, and condiments such as mustard, mayonnaise, ketchup, and relish.

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
  • Condiments: mustard, mayonnaise, ketchup, relish


How to Make

  • Preheat your grill to high heat.
  • In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, onion, egg, bread crumbs, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  • Shape the mixture into 4 patties.
  • Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties for 5 minutes per side, or until well done.
  • If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  • Toast the hamburger buns on the grill until golden brown.
  • Assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a bun and adding desired toppings and condiments.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
